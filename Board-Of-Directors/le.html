Larry Ellison

Biography

  • Born on August 17, 1944, in New York City
  • Co-founder of Oracle Corporation, a global leader in database software
  • Served as Oracle's CEO from 1977 to 2014 and now serves as the CTO and Executive Chairman
  • One of the wealthiest individuals in the world, with a net worth of over $100 billion
  • Awarded the Golden Plate Award of the American Academy of Achievement
  • Known for his competitive sailing endeavors, including winning the America's Cup

Personal Philosophy

Existentialist Philosophy

"There is no single right way to live. It's okay to fail as long as you learn from it. Be passionate about what you do and take risks to achieve greatness."
