David Goggins

Biography

  • Former United States Navy SEAL and United States Air Force Tactical Air Control Party member
  • Known for his ultramarathon running, ultra-distance cycling, and triathlon competitions
  • Author of the best-selling book "Can't Hurt Me: Master Your Mind and Defy the Odds"
  • Holds the Guinness World Record for the most pull-ups done in 24 hours
  • Renowned motivational speaker and advocate for mental toughness and resilience

Personal Philosophy

Buddhist Philosophy of Becoming Reborn

"To grow in life, you have to continuously shed your old self and embrace new challenges. Each day is an opportunity to be reborn, to become a better version of yourself."
