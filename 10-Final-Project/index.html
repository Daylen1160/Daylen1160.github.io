Our mission

Welcome to EZ Shopping! This app is designed to allow users to request items, and for others to be capable of picking them up and delivering them. This allows for communities to become even closer while providing the efficiency and flexibility of shopper's schedules. The app is free to use, with little price increase on items.

    What's offered:

  • Efficient Shopping
  • Community Trust
  • Eco-friendly Practices
  • Accessibility for busy individuals
