What is Valorant?

Valorant is a First Person Shooter (FPS) developed by Riot Games, supposingly being a character-based version of the game Counter-Strike Global Offensive (CSGO). The game consists of 10 players in a 5v5 format on a randomly chosen map, where teams are either attackers or defenders. The objective of the attackers is to either eliminate all the defenders or plant the a bomb (aka the spike) and let it detonate, while defenders attempt to either eliminate all attackers or ensure the bomb is defused. The characters selected in the game are also known as "Agents", all with their own unique set of abilities, creating combinations of abilities to be used together more effectively, maps that cater towards abilites strengths, and possible counter abilities that create weaknesses. Players need to carefully choose a group of agents that work well together, are effective on the map selected, and contribute to the team's effort through fulfilling different roles. Valorant has shown it's dominance in the gaming community since it's released in 2020, catering to an extremely wide demographic of players, only showing promising growth in it's several communities.

Valorant Agents

  1. Brimstone
    Joining from the USA, Brimstone's orbital arsenal ensures his squad always has the advantage. His ability to deliver utility precisely and from a distance make him an unmatched boots-on-the-ground commander.
  2. Phoenix
    Hailing from the UK, Phoenix's star power shines through in his fighting style, igniting the battlefield with flash and flare. Whether he's got backup or not, he'll rush into a fight on his own terms.
  3. Sage
    Sage creates safety for herself and her team wherever she goes. Coming from China, she can revive fallen friends and stave off aggressive pushes, making her a beacon of life in the heat of battle.
  4. Sova
    Sova tracks, finds, and eliminates enemies with ruthless efficiency and precision. His custom bow and incredible scouting abilities ensure that even the most hidden enemies can't escape his sight.
  5. Viper
    Viper deploys an array of poisonous chemical devices to control the battlefield and cripple her enemies' vision. If the toxins don't kill her prey, her mind games surely will.
  6. Cypher
    A one-man surveillance network who keeps tabs on the enemy's every move. The Moroccan information broker, Cypher is always watching. If intel is your game, Cypher is your agent.
  7. Reyna
    Dominating single combat, each kill she gets drastically amplifies her abilities. Originating from Mexico, Reyna has a thirst for battle and devours the life force of her heart.
  8. Killjoy
    The German genius Killjoy secures the battlefield with ease using her arsenal of inventions. If the damage from her gear doesn't stop her enemies, the debuffs her robots provide will help make short work of them.
  9. Breach
    Breach, the bionic Swede, fires powerful, targeted kinetic blasts to aggressively clear a path through enemy ground. The damage and disruption he inflicts ensures no fight is ever fair.
  10. Omen
    A shadowy figure that can appear anywhere, Omen hunts in the darkness. He renders enemies blind, teleports across the field, then lets paranoia take hold as his foes scramble to learn where he might strike next.
  11. Jett
    Representing South Korea, Jett's agile and acrobatic playstyle lets her take risks no one else can. She runs circles around every skirmish, cutting enemies up before they even know what hit them.
  12. Raze
    Raze explodes out of Brazil with her big personality and even bigger explosives. With her satchel charges and grenade launcher, she excels at flushing entrenched enemies and clearing tight spaces with a bang.
  13. Skye
    Hailing from Australia, Skye and her band of beasts trail-blaze the way through hostile territory. With her creations distracting and damaging, they turn the tide of war in her favor with flashes and ferocity.
  14. Yoru
    Japanese native Yoru rips holes straight through reality to infiltrate enemy lines unseen. Using deception and aggression in equal measure, he gets the drop on each target before they know where to look.
  15. Astra
    Astra harnesses the energies of the cosmos to reshape battlefields to her whim. With full command of her astral form and a talent for deep strategic foresight, she's always eons ahead of her enemy's next move.
  16. KAY/O
    KAY/O is a war machine built for a single purpose: neutralizing radiants. His programming is simple - neutralize the enemy. His power to suppress enemy abilities cripples his opponents' capacity to fight back, securing him and his allies the ultimate edge.
  17. Chamber
    Hailing from France, Chamber outsmarts his opponents with deadly precision. His custom-made arsenal is a testament to his classy and lethal playstyle, rendering any enemy unfortunate enough to cross his path defenseless.
  18. Neon
    Neon, from the Philippines, runs fast and hits hard, channeling her electricity into speed and firepower. With lightning-fast reflexes, she dashes across the battlefield, leaving enemies in the dust.
  19. Fade
    Fade, from Turkey, hunts her prey with relentless fervor. With the ability to tap into the fears of her foes, she tracks them down, leaving no place for them to hide.
  20. Harbor
    Originating from India, Harbor commands the power of water to shield his allies and wash away the opposition. He reshapes the battlefield by wielding the might of the ocean itself.
  21. Gekko
    Gekko the Angeleno leads a tight-knit crew of calamitous creatures. His buddies bound forward, scattering enemies out of the way, with Gekko chasing them down to regroup and go again.
  22. Deadlock
    Norwegian operative Deadlock deploys an array of cutting-edge nanowire to secure the battlefield from even the most lethal assault. No one escapes her vigilant watch, nor survives her unyielding ferocity.
  23. Iso
    Chinese fixer for hire, Iso falls into a flow state to dismantle the opposition. Reconfiguring ambient energy into bulletproof protection, he advances with focus towards his next duel to the death.

Maps Overview

Map Name Description
Ascent A map set in Italy, featuring a large open middle area for team fights.
Bind Located on the coast of Morocco, this map has two sites and no middle area, with teleporters connecting different points of the map.
Haven This map is unique for having three bomb sites and is set within a tranquil monastery in Bhutan.
Split Set in Japan, this map is characterized by its narrow choke points and a mid area that splits the map into two sites.
Icebox An industrial port in Russia offering multiple vertical angles and a dynamic environment with zip lines.
Breeze Featuring wide-open spaces and long sightlines, this tropical island map encourages long-range encounters.
Fracture With a H-shaped layout, this map in the American desert features a zip line that spans across the gap dividing the map.
Pearl Pearl is set in an underwater Geodome in Lisbon, Portugal.
Lotus This map is set in a temple-like location in India. It is the second map that contains 3 planting sites.
